Do I need any prior experience?

Not at all. This guide is designed specifically for complete beginners. We explain everything from what a podcast is to how to record your first episode—no prior experience with audio, editing, or publishing required.

How much equipment do I need to buy?

You can start with as little as a $75 USB microphone and headphones you probably already own. The guide includes specific product recommendations for every budget level, from beginner to intermediate.

What format is the guide in?

The main guide is a beautifully designed PDF that you can read on any device, print at home, or keep on your computer for reference. Premium Bundle includes additional downloadable templates in editable formats.

How long until I can publish my first episode?

Most people can go from zero to published episode within 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly they work through the material and prepare their content. The guide is structured so you can move at your own pace.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If this guide doesn't help you launch your podcast, contact us within 30 days for a full refund—no questions asked, no hard feelings.

Will I make money from my podcast?

We include a chapter on monetization that sets realistic expectations. Most podcasts don't make significant revenue for 1-2 years. This guide focuses on helping you launch and build an audience first—that's the foundation for any monetization strategy.
